What is the problem with Authorization?

Authorization is not treated as a system, it's often implicit, convoluted, sprikled throughout the codebase

in our ACL layer ❤️
in our application layer...
in our repositories!

Ok big deal, what if I have auth spread all over? Maybe I like it this way

  • audit
  • testability
  • multi-entity
  • custom roles
